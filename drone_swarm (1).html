🚁 Welcome to the Drone Swarm Simulator

Watch 20 autonomous drones search a collapsed building for 8 trapped survivors. Here is what you will see and how to use it.

1

The map — a collapsed building seen from above

Each square is one section of the building. As drones fly through a room, it lights up bright blue.

Black = wall
Dark = not searched yet
Bright = searched
Yellow dot = survivor
Red square = entry door
2

The drones — tiny quadcopters flying around

Each drone has 4 spinning rotors and a small battery bar. They start at the red entry doors, spread out, and try to search every room. The glowing coloured overlay is their pheromone trail — a shared signal that helps them avoid re-searching the same areas. Blue glow = light traffic, yellow/red = very busy corridor.

3

How to use it

▶ Launch Drones — starts the simulation. The bar at the bottom of the map tells you what is happening at each moment.

Speed slider — drag right to fast-forward.

💥 Debris — drop a new wall mid-mission and watch drones automatically reroute.

⚡ Run all 3 — silently compare our algorithm vs two simpler ones. AH-ACO-RL always covers more.

4

Draw your own building

Use 🧱 Wall / 🗑 Erase / 🧍 Person then click or drag on the map. The drones adapt to whatever layout you create.
